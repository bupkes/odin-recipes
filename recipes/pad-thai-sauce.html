Pad Thai sauce

Description

This is a basic recipe for pad thai sauce.

Usually goes with a pack of stir-fry veg and some tofu.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp tamari or soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1½ tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1½ tbsp lime juice

Steps

  1. Mix them all up in a bowl.
