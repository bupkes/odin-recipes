White Bread

Description

This is a basic recipe for a loaf of white bread.

I usually cook these in a 'dutch oven'.

Ingredients

  • 500g strong white flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 7g sachet fast-action yeast
  • 300g water

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven (with the dutch oven in there) as hot as it will go
  2. Mix the flour, yeast and salt in a big bowl
  3. Mix in the water
  4. Knead it well
  5. Form it into a ball and stick it (carefully - hot!!) into the dutch oven
  6. Put the dutch oven (with lid on) into the oven
  7. Cook for maybe an hour (?)
